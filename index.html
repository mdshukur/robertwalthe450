Thatched Barn Kruzen

Dorfstrasse 6a, 21483 Kruzen, Germany

The barn was built in 1890 and originally served as a dwelling for cattle. In the 1990s the barn was thatched and converted into an old-style house. Today it offers us as a family enough space to live and work.

Apartment under thatch

For 1-2 people, 30m²

Sleeping in an alcove (alcove bed) is a unique experience that you will find in our holiday apartment. Our stylishly furnished holiday apartment should serve as a retreat for you on your journey. It offers everything you need for a short stay.

Your host

Welcome to our beautiful thatched cottage! Stay in one of the most authentic homes in the region and escape the city to enjoy nature. Whether you want to relax, hike through the meadows or cycle along the Elbe, you'll find what you need here. We are located near the beautiful old town of Lauenburg, where you can step back in time with a walk through historic half-timbered architecture. living and sleeping area

The 43-inch TV gets all the usual German channels via IPTV.
